Welcome to my website!
My name is Solveig Greene and my goal for this website is to provide you with information about my career as a teacher and programs that I am currently enrolled in that I am hoping will further my career.
When going through my website you will visit an about me page that will include a short overview of my accomplishments, my resume, a photo of myself, and a link to a short video.
Then, you can visit my page about the programs that I am in and completion dates. I also have a page that discusses my reflections on my career and a page that includes my contact information.
My hope is that you will gain information about me as an employee and recognize my commitment to my employer, my students and my personal growth.

Objectives for this website are:
Recognize the commitment I have to my students.
Identify the dedication I have to my own personal growth.
Determine if my qualifications meet your needs.

I am an elementary teacher.
I have been teaching for 14 years. One of the greatest parts about being a teacher is recognizing the importance of being a lifelong learner.
I truly believe that we should all be constantly investing in our own learning throughout our lives.
Expanding our knowledge and mindsets allow us to become the best that we can become.
During my career, I have invested myself into many learning opportunities to expand my knowledge.
Throughout this website, I hope you are able to identify that learning is my passion and that I have the ability to learn anything that I put my mind to.
I hope you enjoy looking around my website to learn more about me.
