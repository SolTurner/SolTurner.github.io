Specialist Degree Program

In the spring of 2020, I was accepted to the Learning Technologies and Design Educational Specialist degree program by the University of Missouri. My anticipated graduation date is Spring of 2022. The program requires 30 graduate credit hours. The program is designed to train students in the following areas: design learning environments; develop learning systems apps; understand usability; user experience; new technology and communication; improve workplace performance; learn best practices for needs assessment; task analysis, and course design and development. I have included more information on the degree page.

Leadership Program

At the beginning of the 2020-2021 school year, I was accepted by the East Baton Rouge Parish school board to be part of the 2020-2021 Instructional Leader for Tomorrow Cohort IX. The program is for inspiring leaders. Although, I have not completed the program yet, my anticipated completion date is June 2021. I have included more details on the leadership page.
