Specialist Degree


I chose this degree so that I could increase my knowledge of technology as it plays such a vital role in education. I hope to gain knowledge of the competencies so that I can broaden my expertise in education. The program has already taught me so many skills that I would not have even known how to begin learning on my own. Even the instruction in my classroom has improved as I have learned how to effectively analyze the needs of my students and implement best practices to improve student outcomes. I have widened my knowledge of technology platforms and how to use technology in the classroom to improve instruction.The overarching goals of the program are listed below. I have also included some artifacts that demonstrate my ability to meet the goals of the program.

Goals of Learning Technologies Program

Goal 1: To provide students with experiences to develop theory-grounded and research-based competencies for the innovative, aesthetic, effective and sustainable design/development and management of technologies for learning opportunities and systems.
Goal 2: To provide students with the critical, data-collection, analytical and evaluative skills to reflect on and apply to the design/development of learning opportunities and systems.
Goal 3: To provide students with experiences to become socially responsible, reflective/sensitive on the interaction of technologies & society and act ethically in response to current and future challenges of emerging technologies for learning.
