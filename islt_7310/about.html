    • About Me

    My name is Solveig Greene. I am a teacher, wife, and mom. I have two beautiful boys of my own and one amazing step-son. The boys in my house keep me super busy. We enjoy walking, riding bikes, and taking care of our dog, chickens and bees. I live in Baton Rouge, Lousiana. The weather is almost always hot but we still spend most of our time outdoors.


    I am an elementary teacher. I have a bachelor's degree from LSU in elementarty education and a Master's degree from Southeastern University in Educational Leadership. I have been teaching for 14 years. One of the greatest parts about being a teacher is recognizing the importance of being a lifelong learner. I truly believe that we should all be constantly investing in our own learning throughout our lives. Expanding our knowledge and mindsets allow us to become the best that we can become. During my career, I have invested myself into many learning opportunities to expand my knowledge. Throughout this website, I hope you are able to identify that learning is my passion and that I have the ability to learn anything that I put my mind to. I hope you enjoy looking around my website to learn more about me. Please be sure to check out my video and resume below. Happy Learning!


    "Never Stop Learning; For When We Stop Learning, We Stop Growing" -Jack Lewman
